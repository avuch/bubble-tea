The History of Boba Tea

Boba tea was first created in the 1980s in Taiwan. The original version was a simple milk tea served with chewy tapioca pearls, which added a fun and unique texture.

Fun Facts

Did you know that boba tea is also known as pearl milk tea or bubble tea? The name "bubble tea" comes from the frothy bubbles that form when the tea is shaken, not from the tapioca pearls.

Popular Flavors

Boba tea has many popular flovors to choose from, including classic milk tea, taro milk tea, and matcha milk tea.

Types of Boba Tea

There are many types of boba tea to explore. The classic boba milk tea remains a favorite, but there are also fruit teas, slushies, and even coffee-based boba drinks.

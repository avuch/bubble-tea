Classic Milk Tea

A Timeless Favorite

The Classic Milk Tea, made with black tea and milk, is the original boba flavor. It's rich, creamy, and perfectly sweetened.

Brown Sugar Milk Tea

A Caramel Delight

Brown Sugar Milk Tea is a trendy option, known for its rich, caramel-like flavor. The brown sugar syrup is mixed with fresh milk and boba, creating a sweet and satisfying drink.

Taro Milk Tea

Earthy and Sweet

Taro Milk Tea is made from taro root, giving it a unique, earthy flavor and a vibrant purple color. It's sweet, creamy, and loved for its distinctive taste.

Matcha Milk Tea

A Green Tea Twist

Matcha Milk Tea combines the bold, grassy notes of matcha with creamy milk, offering a refreshing and energizing drink that's perfect for green tea lovers.

Jasmine Green Milk Tea

Floral and Refreshing

Jasmine Green Milk Tea has a light, floral flavor thanks to the jasmine-infused green tea. It's less creamy than other milk teas, making it a refreshing choice.

Strawberry Milk Tea

Fruity and Creamy

Strawberry Milk Tea blends the sweetness of strawberries with creamy milk, creating a deliciously fruity and creamy drink that's popular among boba enthusiasts.

Mango Fruit Tea

Tropical Sweetness

Mango Fruit Tea is a vibrant and sweet option made with fresh mango juice and tea. It's fruity, refreshing, and perfect for those who prefer a lighter, non-dairy drink.

Lychee Fruit Tea

Floral and Fruity

Lychee Fruit Tea offers a unique, sweet, and slightly floral flavor. It's made with lychee syrup and tea, providing a refreshing and exotic taste.

Passion Fruit Green Tea

Bright and Citrusy

Passion Fruit Green Tea is a tangy and refreshing option, combining the zesty flavor of passion fruit with the lightness of green tea. It's perfect for a sunny day.
